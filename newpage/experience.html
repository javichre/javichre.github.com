Live the Experience Servicio Para la Paz (SPP) promotes the participation of national and international volunteers by facilitating spaces for work, reflection, critical dialogue, leadership development and action. This contributes to the comprehensive personal development and education of our volunteers, bringing people closer to the social reality of their communities.





International Volunteer experience.
Volunteers visiting our partner communities in the DR learn about community-driven development, and contribute to projects that have been identified by the local community council. Volunteers live in their host community, dedicating their time to volunteer work and learning together with community members. This intentional immersion experience is designed to expose volunteers to the long-term community development process.

Servicio Para la Paz’ Volunteer Corps - Santo Domingo.
Established in 2019, our local volunteer team promotes the culture of community service among youth in Santo Domingo. These volunteers become more active members of their communities, displaying the benefits of having an involvement in service.
More information












Experience for the Development
Some of our activities will take place in strategic locations of the Dominican Republic. A day of community service or visits to natural parks and reserves will serve to promote the economy of the communities we visit, as well as in the communities where Servicio Para la Paz collaborates to support community-driven development.
