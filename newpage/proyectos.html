Our Projects




“Community-Driven Development: Generating Sustainability and Building Peace”.
Servicio Para la Paz (SPP) follows the Community-Driven Development Model for our community projects. This model allows communities to achieve the organization and unity of their community members, working as a team and identifying the needs that each community has. After community councils identify those needs, SPP assists communities in developing and implementing strategies to meet their own objectives, achieving self-sufficiency. This project seeks to empower community councils and local community leaders to work together to meet their own internal needs.



Clean Water and Sanitation.
Servicio Para la Paz (SPP) promotes equitable and communal access to clean water at an affordable price for all. We seek to increase the efficient use of water resources in all sectors, ensuring our communities have access to a sustainable supply of fresh water. Two of these projects include Empowerment H2O, and The Water Harvest Project.



Empowerment H2O We are providing water access to a community through the construction of a 5000 gallon water tank, with the installation of a submersible water pump.



Water Harvest Project This provides a system to capture and store rainwater as a complementary part of the H2O Empowerment Project. By giving community members the opportunity to store rainwater, we support our mission to provide sustainable water access for the families we partner with.
